Ultimate Frisdog

How To Play

You're out at the dog park with your human and want to show off your frisbee-catching skills despite your short legs!

Catch as many frisbees as possible or else your human will want to go home. Avoid other dogs and obstacles in the dog park as you run, but don't let it fly out of the dog park or a car will run over your precious toy.

You have three chances not to collide with any obstacles. If the frisbee flies out of the dog park, it's game over!
